I'm an experienced, creative, and unconventional luthier. For me, each guitar is a generator of hits, ideas, magic, but also a teacher, a good friend and medicine for all kinds of ailments.

I perfected my craft for years by working in one of the best music stores in Poland - Skład Muzyczny. If you bought or serviced a guitar at this store between February 2014 and June 2021, it was me who worked hard to make your instrument perfect. During that period I improved the quality of life of over 4200 guitars.

In August 2021 I started a new chapter in my luthier life and started my own business.

Feel free to contact me, cheers.
Patryk Pater

- the right to limit data processing - when the conditions set out in art. 18 of the GDPR to limit the processing of personal data, the data subject may indicate that there are no grounds for further processing, and the data subject requests to suspend operations on this data or not to delete the data;
- the right to rectify data - at any time, when the need arises, the data subject may inform about the change of their personal data;
- the right to object - at any time you can object to the processing of data in an automated manner, including profiling, as well as object to the processing of data for marketing purposes.
